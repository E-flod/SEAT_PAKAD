What is Seat Pakad?

Seat Pakad is a platform where users can request train ticket bookings. Instead of constantly checking availability, our experienced booking agent monitors seats and books the ticket at the right time as per the user’s request.

How It Works

  • User submits a train ticket booking request
  • Our agent continuously monitors seat availability
  • Ticket is booked at the right time
  • User receives booking confirmation
